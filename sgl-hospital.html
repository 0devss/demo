SGL Hospital

S.G.L Charitable Hospital, founded by H.H. Swami Baba Kashmira Singh Ji started its normal functioning during 1995 with 50 bed capacity. This hospital has been established in the area of Village Garha Jalandhar, to provide medical facilities to the poor and down trodden section of society, without discretion of caste, creed and religion. Patients from local and surrounding districts come in daily who are provided expert medical facilities on very nominal charge. With the constant and strenuous singular efforts of Swami Baba Kashmira Singh Ji, this hospital has now attained the position of a Super Specialty Hospital with the capacity of 225 beds. A team of 24 highly qualified and experienced doctors.

Department of Medicine, Surgery (General and Laparoscopic), Gastroenterology, Orthopedics, Ophthalmology, Gynae. & Obst., Pediatrics, Orthodentistary , ENT, Anaesthesiology, Dermatology, Pathology, Radiology, De-Addiction and Physiotherapy & also Super Specialty Wing, Nephrology, Urology, Surgical Gastroenterology, Laparoscopic Surgery & Gastrointestinal Endoscopy/ERCP., Vitreoretinal Surgery, Joint Replacement are managed by reputed and highly qualified doctors with fame.

Our Commitment to the Community



Free Medical Camps

We regularly organize medical camps in underserved communities, offering general consultations and essential medicines — at free of cost.


Free Mammography

Poor and needy masses can avail the facilities of free check of cancer with mammography (by paying the cost of film).


Free Dialysis for Needy Patients

Dialysis is a life-saving treatment — and we're proud to offer free dialysis (only cost of disposables is taken) sessions to low-income patients suffering from chronic kidney disease, thanks to donor contributions.


Donation-Based Surgeries

We perform life-changing surgeries for patients who cannot afford them — entirely funded by our donation partners.
