Courses Offered

M.Sc. Nursing

M.Sc. Nursing is a 2-year postgraduate course that offers specialization in fields like Medical-Surgical, Pediatric, Psychiatric, and Community Health Nursing. It is designed for graduate nurses aiming for advanced clinical roles, teaching positions, or research opportunities.

B.Sc. Nursing

B.Sc. Nursing is a 4-year undergraduate program that focuses on theoretical and practical training in areas such as medical-surgical nursing, community health, pediatrics, and more. Graduates are eligible to register as professional nurses and pursue higher studies.

GNM Nursing

GNM is a 3.5-year diploma course that trains students in clinical nursing and midwifery skills. It equips learners with the knowledge and hands-on experience required to provide patient care in hospitals, clinics, and community settings. This course offers a strong foundation for a nursing career.

Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing

Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing is a 2-year degree program for registered GNM nurses seeking to upgrade their qualifications. It builds on existing skills and provides advanced knowledge in nursing, preparing graduates for leadership and specialized roles in healthcare.
