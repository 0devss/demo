Check Requirements for Working in Germany


✅ Recognized Nursing Qualification

You must have a recognized nursing qualification: GNM (General Nursing and Midwifery), B.Sc. Nursing, or Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing.


✅ Anerkennung

Get Your Qualifications Recognized in Germany (Anerkennung). This is a mandatory process to work legally as a nurse in Germany.


✅ German Language

Minimum B1 (intermediate) level is required, but B2 is usually preferred by employers and often required for recognition.


✅ Employment Visa

You must have employment visa which Requires B1/B2 German, job contract or training confirmation.


✅ Get Registered and Start Working

After successful recognition and language requirements, You will be registered as a licensed nurse & can start working in hospitals, elderly care homes, or clinics.
