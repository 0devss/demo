About Us

About Us


We are a German-based recruitment agency with Indian roots specialized in placing Indian nurses and healthcare professionals in Germany. With strong ties to German hospitals, clinics, and elderly care facilities, we bridge the gap between skilled nurses seeking rewarding careers and German employers searching for dedicated staff. We understand that starting a career abroad – or finding qualified staff from another country – can be overwhelming. That’s why we’ve made it our mission to simplify this process for both sides.

Our Story


Our journey began with personal experience. We made firsthand experience how the shortage of qualified nurses impacts the patients caretaking and increases the stress on the system. As we have deep connection to Indian nursing universities and hospitals, we saw an opportunity to solve this challenge in Germany and increase the life of qualified nurses from India seeking new opportunities by connecting the two.

As founders with Indian and German roots, we bring together two perspectives. Through our strong and long-standing connections to India, we have deep insight into the high level of nursing education and the motivation of many well-qualified professionals seeking international opportunities. At the same time, having lived in Germany for decades, we know the country, its healthcare system and the high quality of life it offers.

We founded our company to address a problem we personally understand – by building a bridge between qualified nurses in India and healthcare providers in Germany. Our goal is not simply recruitment, but sustainable integration. We want to make Germany an attractive and long-term destination for nurses by highlighting professional stability, social security, and quality of life – so they choose Germany over alternatives like the UK or Canada, where working and living conditions are often less favorable.

Our mission is driven by responsibility, transparency, and respect for the people behind every placement. By combining cultural understanding, professional standards, and personal commitment, we aim to create lasting solutions for Germany’s healthcare system—and real opportunities for the nurses who support it every day.

Our Core Values


We believe in Humanity, Responsibility and Quality. Our caregivers come from an environment based on respect, education and care – financed and supported by a non-profit network. For us, it's not just qualification that counts – but also character.

With these values, we face the shortage of skilled workers in nursing and want to play our part in solving it. By 2030, there will be a shortage of up to 200,000 skilled nurses in Germany. Hospitals, doctors and nursing facilities are overwhelmed. Help can come from abroad.

Through our partnership with the renowned nursing schools and the hospitals like the SGL in Punjab (India), we offer certified, top-trained nurses – fully supported up to the first day of work and beyond in Germany.



Together, we place the right hearts in the right places


All members of RMC Nurses share the same values and work on a shared vision to make the world a better place.
