About Us

About Us


We are an international recruitment agency specializing in placing Indian nurses and healthcare professionals in Germany. With strong ties to German hospitals, clinics, and elder care facilities, we bridge the gap between skilled nurses seeking rewarding careers and German employers searching for dedicated staff. We understand that starting a career abroad — or finding qualified staff from another country — can be overwhelming. That’s why we’ve made it our mission to simplify this process for both sides.

Our Core Values


We believe in Humanity, Responsibility and Quality. Our caregivers come from an environment based on respect, education and care – financed and supported by a non-profit network. For us, it's not just qualification that counts – but also character.

With these values, we face the shortage of skilled workers in nursing and want to play our part in solving it. By 2030, there will be a shortage of up to 200,000 skilled workers. Hospitals, doctors and nursing facilities are overwhelmed. Help can come from abroad.

Through our partnership with the renowned SGL Nursing School and the SGL Hospital in Punjab (India), we offer certified, top-trained nurses – fully supported up to the first day of work and beyond in Germany.



Together, we place the right hearts in the right places


All members of RMC Nurses share the same values and work on a shared vision to make the world a better place.
